TickerTrader

TickerTrader is a program that enables stock trading on the command line using an Alpaca brokerage account. I was dissatisfied with the amount of time it would take me to place orders when scalping very short term. In those scenarios even a few seconds matters and having to go through a web interface was just not cutting it. So, TickerTrader was born and its designed to need the smallest amount of input possible in order to make trades and even allows preset data to further reduce the amount of input needed for each trade.
