About Me



My name is Danny Mickens. I am 25 and living in North Carolina. In an attempt to not get any other personal location/contact information harvested by bots I will leave it at that.

I started creating this website for a few reasons. One, as a developer I've always shyed away from anything front end related, worked out of a terminal, and focused on function over anything else. This site is helping me sharpen (or lets be honest, create for the first time) my web development skills.

Two, from time to time over the course of this pandemic I've put in some hours building tools to help me in my trading endeavors. These are public and I want others to be able to access them. On this site I will have links to my github repositories and use case descriptions.

Lastly, I want this to serve as a portal supporting my career move to software development. Along with my public projects I will host a in depth page for my resume coupled with a downloadable link. I want this site to show both my personality and my knowledge and experience to show how I may fit into the culture of a new workplace.
